## Books

This list might also be more up to date than the Amazon one... who even knows. This list was originally inspired (and populated) after [reading this article](https://uxplanet.org/50-best-resources-for-ui-ux-professionals-c29daf36cd85), but has been augmented with recommendations from friends, UX and dev professionals, people who have interviewed me, and others.
